rubik's cube

intro to rubik's cube

  • history

    • invented in 1974 by Hungarian sculptor and professor of architecture Ernő Rubik
    • originally called the "Magic Cube"

  • difficult?

    • in July 2010, a team of researchers, working with Google, proved the so-called "God's number" to be 20

variations

  • 2×2×2 (Pocket/Mini Cube)
  • 4×4×4 (Rubik's Revenge/Master Cube)
  • 5×5×5 (Professor's Cube)
  • 6×6×6 (V-Cube 6)
  • 7×7×7 (V-Cube 7)

more variations

start learning

  • core concept

    • face by face
    • layer by layer

  • basic moves

    • R, R'(Ri) --> right face
    • L, L'(Li) --> left face
    • U, U'(Ui) --> up face
    • D, D'(Di) --> down face
    • F, F'(Fi) --> front face
    • B, B'(Bi) --> back face

first layer

  • try to solve by yourself
  • first, try to solve a face
  • then, try solving a complete layer

second layer

  • in fact, there's only 4 pieces
  • URU'R'U'F'UF
  • U'L'ULUFU'F'

third layer(1)

  • before the "cross" appear

    • FRUR'U'F'
    • fRUR'U'f'

  • before the top face is complete

    • RUR'URUUR'
    • L'U'LU'L'U'U'L

third layer(2)

  • switch corner

    • RB'RF2R'BRF2R2
    • L'BL'F2LB'L'F2L'2

  • switch side

    • F2UR'LF2RL'UF2
    • F2U'R'LF2RL'U'F2
